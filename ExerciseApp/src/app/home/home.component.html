About This App

This browser based app is used to track and organized your workout information into a readable table-based format. With it, you can track the various types of workouts, the number of repetitions per set of the workout, and the number of sets you've performed as well as the date when they performed on. Your fitness goals have never been easier to achieve! Just enter the details of your workout into your workout log after logging in, and the website will save it for you when you return!

Date Tracking

Log the date of your workouts and have them sorted chronologically, so you can see how you've done over the course of days, weeks, months, and years!

Workout Logging

Log workouts you've performed and have them organized into an easy-to-read format for you!

Share with Friends

Share your fitness progress with friends on various social media providers, such as Facebook and Google plus!

Head over to the login page to begin tracking your fitness progress and managing your profile!
